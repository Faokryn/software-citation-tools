Risks

Risk Name Risk Severity Risk Probability Description
Falling Further behind High Medium A lot of the documentation depends on prior documentation that we didn't complete on time because of our late start. If we don't catch up on those docs, we won't be able to complete the later docs either and fall further behind instead of catching up.
Scope Creep Very High High Given the loose nature of the project and it's basis on us eliciting requirements from end users instead of from a customer, we will face a lot of potential scope creep in our project.
Poor Community Engagement High Medium It's clearly very important to both us and our sponsor that we gather community engagement on our project not only for requirement gathering but also to continue the project on after we have left to ensure it remains useful and relevant to the community.
